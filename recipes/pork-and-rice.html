Pork-n-Rice

This is a tasty pork and rice dish. I found this recipe on Tiktok and it fucks.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork mince
  • 3 potatos, diced
  • 5 bulbs of garlic
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 green and 1 red capsicum
  • Some dark and some light soy sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Szechuan peppercorns to taste

Method

Stir fry that jawn however you like. Add the seasonings to taste. Dice and soak the potatos for a half hour before cooking is all.
