Gochujang Steak and Rice

Yooooo this is a nice one. Spicy and sweet and delightful, pretty sure this one is Tiktok's too.

Ingredients

  • Steak of choice
  • Gochujang
  • Honey
  • Rice
  • Broccolini or similar

Da Method

  1. First, you're gonna want to wash and then steam your rice. Any method is fine.
  2. While that's happening, marinate your steak in a mix of Gochujang and honey.
  3. BBQ these jawns with the greens.
  4. Eat.
